Afternoon

Day 2: Haldi & Wedding

Haldi Ceremony

Morning

+

Traditional turmeric ceremony for blessings. A paste made from turmeric, sandalwood, and other ingredients is applied to the bride and groom to bless them before the wedding.

Dress Code: Yellow clothes recommended (clothes will get stained!)

Wedding Ceremony

Evening

+

The main wedding ceremony - don't miss it! This is when Mahathi and Rahul will tie the knot in a traditional ceremony with family and friends.

Note: Please be punctual as the ceremony follows traditional auspicious timing.
