Will food be provided?

Yes! All meals during the wedding days (20th-22nd Feb) will be provided for all guests.

Yes, accommodation will be arranged for all guests for the nights of 20th and 21st February.

Yellow clothes are recommended for the Haldi ceremony as it's a traditional turmeric ceremony and your clothes might get stained with turmeric!
