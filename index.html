Wedding Schedule

Three days of celebration

20 Feb

Day 1: Mehendi & Sangeet

Mehendi Ceremony

Afternoon

+

Traditional henna ceremony with beautiful designs. A fun and colorful event where intricate henna patterns are applied to the bride's hands and feet.

Sangeet Night

Evening

+

Musical night filled with dance, music, and celebrations. The perfect time to showcase your dancing skills!

Want to perform? Feel free to prepare performances! Let us know if you'd like to be part of the show.

21 Feb

Day 2: Haldi & Wedding

Haldi Ceremony

Morning

+

Traditional turmeric ceremony for blessings. A paste made from turmeric, sandalwood, and other ingredients is applied to the bride and groom to bless them before the wedding.

Dress Code: Yellow clothes recommended (clothes will get stained!)

Wedding Ceremony

Evening

+

The main wedding ceremony - don't miss it! This is when Mahathi and Rahul will tie the knot in a traditional ceremony with family and friends.

Note: Please be punctual as the ceremony follows traditional auspicious timing.

22 Feb

Day 3: Reception

Wedding Reception

Evening

+

Celebration dinner and reception. Join us for a grand evening of food, drinks, and festivities as we celebrate the newly married couple!

Dress Code: Formal/Semi-formal Indian or Western attire
