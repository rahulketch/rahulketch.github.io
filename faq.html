Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

When should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP by November 1, 2025 to help us with planning and arrangements.

Will food be provided?

Yes! All meals during the wedding days (20th-22nd Feb) will be provided for all guests.

Is accommodation provided?

Yes, accommodation will be arranged for all guests for the nights of 20th and 21st February.

What should I wear to the Haldi ceremony?

Yellow clothes are recommended for the Haldi ceremony as it's a traditional turmeric ceremony and your clothes might get stained with turmeric!

Can I perform at the Sangeet?

Absolutely! We'd love to see performances from our friends and family. Please let us know if you'd like to perform so we can coordinate the schedule.

Yes! We're organizing a post-wedding trip. Details are not final yet, but we'll share information soon. Check the "Post-Wedding Trip" section above!

How do I get to the venue?

Jenvi Conventions is located in Hyderabad. Detailed directions and transportation information will be shared with you directly.

Will you hate me if I don't come to the wedding?

Of course not! We totally understand and we love you.
